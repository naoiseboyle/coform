Our Services

Research

Research is at the core of what we do to understand your customers and the business problem. We drive research efforts through a range of qualitative and evaluative methods focusing on the local specific context to uncover insights and grow understanding.

Product development

Whether creating an MVP or improving an existing solution we help innovate and improve your product to be the best it can be. Our product development services cover all aspects of design driven product development including User Experience and UI Design, Prototyping and testing, Quality Assurance and Measurement.

Digital transformation

Building a design driven mindset and capabilities is key to implementing a design orientated innovative business. We can help you with practical advice and coaching to introduce a design mindset and workflows to your company. Our mentoring services range from how to introduce a DesignOps practice to facilitation and workshops for digital innovation.

Our Process

Discover

By understanding users, stakeholders and the surrounding environment we can build up a bigger picture of the problem space. We use several design research methods from interviews to competitor analysis to discover and understand how we can best address the challenge.

Define

After gaining clarity to the problem space we synthesise our observations to identify the core aspects we have uncovered. By synthesising findings we can create hypothesis, define our shared perspectives and set clear goals moving forward.

Ideate

No idea is too wild, by using our creativity to explore outlandish solutions we can arrive at truly unique solutions. Exploring several ideas allows us to embrace plurality and possibilities.

Prototype

Prototyping is a key aspect of any good design process. Prototypes help realise our ideas in the leanest way possible so we can test and validate our ideas and progress to solutions that deliver most impact.

Measure

Understanding what makes something successful is paramount to considering current and future success. We always set our metrics up front and measure where we are meeting needs and where we can improve.
